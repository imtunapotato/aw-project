## Context and Problem Statement
Avoid complexity in the VacaProducts Screen, user-friendliness
## Decision Drivers
Ensure the ability to change system parts without affecting others.
In this case, searching for different meat, or glancing at the featured/trending meat
## Decision Outcome
Two microfrontends (besides header & footer), one for viewing the featured meat,
and another to search the different types of meat available.
### Consequences
* Good: Appealing and Simple
* Bad:
